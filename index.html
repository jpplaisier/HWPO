Time Cap: 10 Minutes

NOTES:
- The weight on the bar should not require much warm-up at all. You should be physically capable of cycling this weight for touch-and-go reps when fresh, even if you choose to do quick singles for this AMRAP. If not, then scale back to ~45kg.
- Use the burpees as your "throttle." If you feel good, move faster or jump up from each rep, and if you don't, try to slow the movement down.
- Remember that in order to finish under the time cap, your rounds must take less than one minute on average. Scale accordingly, or get as far as you can until the time cap!
