Bonus

30-45:00 Bike Erg @ Zone 2 Heart Rate; 80-90 RPM

NOTES:
- Your Zone 2 Heart Rate Range: 60bpm to 70bpm
- The goal here is to stay in a specific heart rate zone, not specifically about total output or distance. For many people, Zone 2 efforts will feel embarrassingly slow, and that is okay. This work helps build a larger aerobic base at lower intensity, so be sure you are a few clicks below your max output for this time frame.
- If you're working without a reliable heart rate monitor, aim to stay at an effort where breathing is controlled (nasal breathing can help), and you can speak in full sentences without strain. If you're gasping or needing to shorten sentences, then you are likely working above Zone 2.
