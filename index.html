GENERAL / MISC.
- This workout is a repeat from CrossFit Open 22.3. Be sure to refer back to any old notes, data, or video you may have if you have done this before!
- The optimal strategy for this workout will depend on the type of athlete you are. Athletes who are limited by the barbell should look to manage the gymnastics, and the opposite is true for athletes who are limited by the gymnastics.
- Only athletes who are confidently fighting for a top 1% score on this metcon should attempt to stay fast and unbroken from the start. Even then, strategic breaks from the start may be helpful in maximizing their score.
- In 2022, only ~12% of women and ~22% of men finished under the cap. Consider that the majority of athletes participating in this workout will likely not finish the prescribed work under the 12:00 time cap.
- For those aiming for a top 1-2% finish, know the times may need to be faster in 2025 than what was required in 2022. Check out this article for data from 2022: https://games.crossfit.com/article/223-workout-analysis/open
- If you are trying to qualify to Semifinals, be sure to read through and adhere to all video submission standards at: https://games.crossfit.com/workouts/open/2025/2
- If you have time to do so, check out the member call video linked below to hear Jake D. and Josh's first thoughts over the 25.2!

----

RIG GYMNASTICS.
- Pay attention to how far away from the bar you move if taking breaks on any of the movements on the rig. Ideally, any rest should be right under the pull-up bar.
- Aim to stay at least 2-3 reps away from failure until the last reps or last seconds of this workout. Keep some energy in the tank for the later rounds.
- For any athletes fighting for their first bar muscle-up, be sure to save the grip by breaking the earlier gymnastics reps up from the start.

----

DOUBLE-UNDERS.
- These sets can be overlooked, but be sure you feel warm and dialed-in with your technique before starting.
- If needed, a quick intentional break is almost always faster than an unplanned break.

----

THRUSTERS.
- Expect that the final weight will feel heavier by the end of this workout than it would normally feel.
- Only aim for unbroken reps from the start if you are very comfortable with the listed weights and you are not worried about fatigue affecting your gymnastics.
- Similar to the gymnastics movements, pay attention to where you rest here, if you do. Ideally, any breaks will happen without stepping away from the barbell.
