5 Sets:
600m @ 03:38 - 03:43/1000m
200m @ Easy / Recovery Jog
*No Rest Between Sets

NOTES.
- This will be a slight increase in your paces from the last time you did a similar piece in this session. Remember to slow down the recoveries to allow for your true intensity to be in each 600m effort. This should be two very distinct paces between the two distances.
- If you don't have a current 5K pace, try to maintain a consistent hard pace across each 600m that you can get back to after the 200m recovery.
