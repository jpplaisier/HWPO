bonus

Row Intervals.
------------
4 Sets:
6:00 Row @ 02:22/500m; 22 S/M
Rest 2:00 Between Sets

NOTES:
- The focus of this session is to develop rowing stroke technique and power. The low stroke rate will mean the momentum of the flywheel will reduce before making the next stroke, meaning you will need to generate more power through each stroke to get the momentum back up.
- The low stroke rate also allows you to consciously think more about your technique and positioning. Think about driving through your legs with straight arms and only extending your back once the legs have extended. The back will move from a 10 o'clock position at the front of the stroke and extend to a 2 o'clock position at the back of the stroke. Then, simply follow through with relaxed arms until the handle meets your sternum.
- Finally, these longer duration intervals will help build up your tolerance to rowing longer distances and will make the 5K retest feel much more comfortable.
- If you are using a PM5 monitor, use this link to set your erg and track your results on the ErgData app: https://workout.ergdata.com/shared/vdolsnv1
