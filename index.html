Daily Summary powered by ChatGPT

Strength:
5 sets of 2 deadlifts at 83kg with RPE 8, paired with 5/5 single leg box jumps. Then, every 2 minutes for 8 minutes, perform 40 seconds of jumping box step-ups. Rest at least 2 minutes between sets. Maintain control during deadlifts and focus on explosive movements during box jumps.
Cardio:
Retest a 2K row with a primer and main set. Primer consists of 3 minutes at RPE 5-6, followed by a 400m row at target 2K pace, and a 200m row faster than target 2K pace. The main set is a 2000m row for time. Aim to row aggressively at the start, settle into a pace, and finish with a sprint. Afterward, cool down with a 500m row at an easy pace.
Accessory:
Perform 4 sets of 20 seated hamstring curls at RPE 8, with at least 1 minute of rest between sets. Then, do 4 sets of 15 reverse hypers at 30-40kg with RPE 7-8, resting at least 1 minute between sets.
