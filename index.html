Time Cap: 12 Minutes

NOTES:
- This metcon starts to compound on itself with the fatigue that will set in the deeper you get. Find a pace that pushes you hard from the start but still allows you to always transition straight into the next movement.
- If you have not had much exposure to the GHD sit-up, consider breaking these reps up or even scaling the amount of reps, range of motion, or both. This can be a potent movement, especially for those without much experience on the GHD.
- If you don't have pistols, scale to either box step-ups or air squats for double the reps.
- For the clusters, the weight should be moderate. You might be able to cycle this load when fresh or for the last few reps, but in order to stay moving, quick singles will likely be the fastest approach for the majority of the reps.
