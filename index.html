NOTES:
- Use the first 5:00 to settle into a smooth and sustainable pace, with a focus on technique. Once you get into the 100's, be sure to hold nothing back from the start. Cut this off after six sets or seven sets if you are struggling to maintain anything close to a sprint pace.
- Even in the sprints, focus on maintaining proper row mechanics: legs first, then a lean of the torso to 11 o'clock, and finish through with the arms.
- The goal of this session is to improve your overall power on the machine and build in good habits for powerful starts.
